Introduction簡介

What is
AI Gender Bias?

什麼是
AI 性別偏見？

AI systems learn from vast amounts of human-generated data. When that data reflects historical gender inequalities, AI models absorb and reproduce those biases — often invisibly and at scale.

AI 系統從大量人類產生的資料中學習。當這些資料反映了歷史上的性別不平等，AI 模型就會吸收並重現這些偏見——往往是在無形中大規模擴散。

This matters because AI increasingly shapes who gets hired, what content we see, and how healthcare is diagnosed. Biased AI doesn't just reflect inequality — it amplifies it.

這至關重要，因為 AI 越來越廣泛地影響求職、內容推薦，甚至醫療診斷。有偏見的 AI 不只反映不平等，而是放大了它。

Root Causes根本原因

Why Does
Bias Happen?

偏見為何
會發生？

01
Biased Training Data
有偏見的訓練資料
AI learns from historical data generated in societies that practiced gender discrimination. Job listings, literature, and web content overrepresent men in leadership and women in caregiving — AI absorbs this as "normal."
AI 從歷史資料中學習，而那些資料來自長期存在性別歧視的社會。職缺廣告、文學作品與網路內容都過度呈現「男性領導、女性照護」的模式，AI 將此視為「正常」。
02
Lack of Diversity in AI Teams
AI 開發團隊缺乏多元性
The majority of AI researchers and engineers are men. Teams without gender diversity are less likely to notice or prioritize gender issues during design, testing, and deployment.
AI 研究人員和工程師的絕大多數為男性。缺乏性別多元的團隊，在設計、測試與部署過程中，較不容易發現或重視性別相關問題。
03
Optimization Without Fairness
只追求效能，忽視公平性
AI is optimized for accuracy or engagement — not fairness. Without explicit fairness goals, a model can achieve high overall accuracy while performing significantly worse for underrepresented groups.
AI 系統通常以準確率或參與度為優化目標，而非公平性。若沒有明確的公平指標，模型可能整體表現出色，卻對弱勢群體效果明顯較差。
04
Self-Reinforcing Feedback Loops
偏見的自我強化迴圈
When a biased AI shows fewer women a job ad, fewer women apply, generating data suggesting women aren't interested — which reinforces the bias in the next model cycle.
當有偏見的 AI 減少向女性展示職缺廣告，女性申請者變少，進而產生「女性對該領域不感興趣」的資料，再度強化下一個模型的偏見，形成惡性循環。
05
Lack of Regulation & Accountability
缺乏法規與究責機制
In many regions, no laws require bias audits before AI deployment. Companies often lack the incentives — or awareness — to proactively address gender bias in their products.
許多地區沒有法律要求在部署前進行 AI 偏見審查。企業往往缺乏主動解決性別偏見的誘因，甚至根本沒有這方面的意識。

Economic Inequality
經濟不平等
Biased hiring and lending algorithms widen the gender pay gap and reduce women's access to capital.
有偏見的招募與貸款演算法拉大性別薪資差距，並減少女性取得資本的機會。
Women are already paid less. Add an AI hiring tool that quietly filters them out, plus a lending algorithm that offers them smaller loans — and the gap compounds. Technology doesn't fix systemic inequality. It can turbocharge it.
女性的薪資本來就偏低，再加上 AI 招募工具默默篩掉她們、貸款演算法給她們更低的額度——差距只會越來越大。科技本身並不解決結構性不平等，反而可能讓它加速惡化。

Underrepresentation in Leadership
領導層的代表性不足
When AI screens candidates and surfaces news, it can make women less visible in professional and political spheres.
AI 篩選人才與新聞推送時，可能讓女性在職場與政治舞台上更難被看見。
If an AI-powered news feed shows fewer women in leadership roles, readers unconsciously update their sense of what's "normal." If a hiring algorithm ranks male candidates higher, fewer women reach the top. The glass ceiling gets automated.
如果 AI 新聞推送少給你看女性領袖的報導，你對「正常」的感受就會悄悄改變。如果 AI 招募系統給男性更高的分數，女性就更難升到頂端。玻璃天花板，被自動化了。

Health Outcomes
健康結果差異
Medical AI trained on male-skewed data can miss diagnoses in women, worsening already under-recognized conditions.
以男性資料為主訓練的醫療 AI，可能漏診女性患者，加重本就容易被低估的問題。
Heart disease presents differently in women. Depression, chronic pain, autoimmune conditions — all historically under-diagnosed in women. When medical AI trains on male-majority data, it learns to be worse at spotting these. The cost is measured in lives.
心臟病在女性身上的症狀本就不同。憂鬱症、慢性疼痛、自體免疫疾病——這些在女性身上本來就容易被漏診。醫療 AI 拿男性為主的資料訓練，就是在學習如何更差地辨識這些狀況。代價是人命。

Reinforced Stereotypes
強化刻板印象
When AI content consistently reflects gendered assumptions, it normalizes stereotypes for a whole generation.
當 AI 內容持續反映性別假設，整整一個世代的刻板印象就被常態化了。
Children today grow up asking AI questions, getting AI-generated images, reading AI-curated content. If every AI "scientist" is a man and every AI "caregiver" is a woman, that's what feels natural. Bias doesn't need to be loud to do damage.
現在的孩子從小問 AI 問題、看 AI 生成的圖片、讀 AI 篩選的內容。如果 AI 裡的「科學家」都是男性、「照顧者」都是女性，這就變成了「理所當然」。偏見不需要大聲說出來，才能造成傷害。

🌏
Setback for SDG 5
阻礙 SDG 5 的實現
The UN's SDG 5 calls for gender equality by 2030. Unchecked AI bias actively works against this goal — technology meant to be neutral becomes a powerful enforcer of the very inequalities the world is trying to dismantle. Achieving SDG 5 requires deliberately building fairness into every layer of AI.
聯合國 SDG 5 呼籲在 2030 年前實現性別平等。不受約束的 AI 偏見正積極阻礙這個目標——原本應中立的技術，反而成為鞏固不平等的強大工具。在 AI 時代實現 SDG 5，需要在系統的每一層都刻意建立公平性。

How to Improve如何改善

Building
Fairer AI

打造
更公平的 AI

Addressing gender bias in AI requires action from developers, policymakers, organizations, and individuals alike.

解決 AI 中的性別偏見，需要開發者、政策制定者、組織與個人共同行動。

📊
Diverse & Representative Data
多元且具代表性的資料
Audit training datasets for gender imbalance. Actively collect data representing all genders to prevent inherited biases from the past.
審查訓練資料集的性別比例，主動蒐集能代表各性別的資料，避免歷史偏見被繼承下來。
👥
Diverse AI Teams
多元化的開發團隊
Build teams that include women, social scientists, and ethicists. Diverse teams spot bias that homogeneous ones miss.
建立包含女性、社會科學家與倫理學家的跨領域團隊。多元團隊能察覺同質團隊容易忽略的偏見。
⚖️
Fairness-Aware Algorithms
納入公平性的演算法
Design AI with explicit fairness metrics. Use techniques like adversarial debiasing and re-weighting to minimize disparate impact across genders.
在 AI 設計中加入明確的公平性指標，運用對抗式去偏、重新加權等技術，降低對不同性別的差異影響。
🔍
Transparency & Auditing
透明度與獨立稽核
Require regular independent bias audits before AI deployment. Publish results publicly to create accountability and enable community oversight.
要求 AI 部署前進行定期獨立稽核，並公開結果，建立問責機制，讓社會大眾能夠監督。
📜
Policy & Regulation
政策與法規制定
Advocate for laws mandating bias testing in high-stakes AI. The EU AI Act is an early example — more frameworks are needed globally.
倡議立法強制要求高風險 AI 進行偏見測試。歐盟 AI 法案是早期範例，全球需要更多類似框架。
🎓
Education & Awareness
教育與意識提升
Teach AI literacy including gender and ethics so the next generation can critically evaluate and challenge biased systems.
推廣包含性別與倫理議題的 AI 素養教育，讓下一代都能批判性地評估並挑戰有偏見的系統。
